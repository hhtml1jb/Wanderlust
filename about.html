About Wanderlust

Inspiring travelers to explore the world with confidence, curiosity, and respect

Our Story

"To inspire and empower travelers to explore the world authentically, responsibly, and with a deeper understanding of the places and cultures they encounter."

Wanderlust was born from a shared passion for travel and a desire to help others experience the world in meaningful ways. Founded in 2018 by a team of experienced travelers and industry professionals, we set out to create a comprehensive resource that goes beyond typical tourism.

What began as a small blog has grown into a trusted travel platform offering detailed destination guides, expert advice, and authentic perspectives from around the globe. We believe that travel is transformative‚Äîit broadens horizons, challenges preconceptions, and creates lasting connections.

Our mission is to equip travelers with the knowledge, confidence, and resources to embark on journeys that are not only enjoyable but also enriching and responsible.

Our Journey

2018

Where It All Began

Wanderlust launched as a small travel blog focused on authentic travel experiences in Southeast Asia. Our first guides covered Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia.

2019

Expanding Horizons

We expanded our coverage to include destinations across Europe and South America. Our community grew to over 100,000 monthly readers.

2020

Adapting to Change

During the global pandemic, we pivoted to provide virtual travel experiences and resources for future trip planning, helping our community stay connected to the world.

2021

New Partnerships

We formed partnerships with sustainable tourism organizations and local guides worldwide to provide more authentic insider perspectives.

2022

Award Recognition

Wanderlust received recognition as one of the "Top Travel Resources" by Travel + Leisure magazine and expanded our team to include experts from various travel niches.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

We launched our "Travel Better" initiative, focusing on sustainable and responsible travel practices, including partnerships with conservation organizations.

2024

Global Community

Our community expanded to over 1 million monthly readers across 150+ countries, with content available in multiple languages.

2025

The Future

We continue to evolve and grow, with exciting new projects and partnerships on the horizon as we help more travelers explore the world meaningfully.
